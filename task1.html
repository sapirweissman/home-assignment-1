What is a strawberry?

The strawberry is a sweet, small, round-shaped fruit that develops on the plants of the strawberry. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is considered one of the most popular fruits in the world due to its sweet and fresh taste. Strawberries are known for their distinct flavor and pleasant aroma and are used in various dishes, decorations, spreads, desserts, and are also enjoyed fresh as a snack. Strawberries are available in a variety of colors, including white, red, pink, and orange, depending on the specific type. They contain abundant amounts of vitamins, minerals, and important antioxidants that are beneficial for health. Strawberries can be found in a wide variety of countries around the world. They grow in different regions and are present in almost all parts of the world, depending on the suitable growing conditions for them.

Benefits of the strawberry fruit

Strawberries are not only tasty and sweet fruits, but they are also excellent from a nutritional standpoint and contain many health benefits.

  • Strawberries are rich in vitamins and minerals. They contain high amounts of vitamin C.
  • They contain antioxidants that can help prevent damage caused by oxidative stress in the body.
  • Supports Heart Health: Compounds such as flavonoids in strawberries may contribute to improving cardiovascular health and reducing the risk of heart-related diseases.
  • Support for Brain Function: The strawberry hull contains polyphenols linked to improving cognitive functions and brain performance.
  • Easily Available and Accessible: They are readily found in most areas and can be purchased at markets and supermarkets during their season.
  • Support for Digestive Processes: They contain nutritional fibers that can assist in maintaining proper digestion.
  • Low in Calories: Strawberries are among the low-calorie fruits, hence considered a recommended food in a balanced diet menu.
  • May Aid in Weight Management and Skin Health: They contain carbohydrates and fibers that may support weight management and skin health.

Recommended recipe for strawberry whipped cream dessert

Ingredients:

  • 350 grams fresh strawberries
  • 125 grams biscuits (such as petit beurre)
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Preparation Instructions:
  1. Crush the biscuits by hand into various-sized pieces.
  2. Cut the strawberries into small cubes.
  3. Whip the heavy cream with the vanilla extract and powdered sugar until obtaining soft but stable whipped cream. Transfer it to a piping bag.
  4. Assembly: At the bottom of the dish, place 2-3 tablespoons of crushed biscuits, spread a layer of whipped cream, place 3 teaspoons of strawberries. Repeat this process for another 3 layers.
  5. The dessert can be eaten immediately or kept in the refrigerator for up to two days.
